My top 3 movies of all-time.

Karate Kid

It is a nice and interesting movie, it shows the will of a young man and his determination to win against all odds.

Irobot

Will Smith did justice to the movie. A man against robots but he continued to fight till he was victorious.

A Good Day To Die Hard

Bruce Willis really showed the world how action films should be acted. The movie had a good story, nice moves and a never-ending peserverance from the protagonist.
